Khawa Technology is a web development company based in Strasbourg, France that specializes in backend development with open source solutions.

We are available part time for remote work. We can integrate with your existing team or take the technical lead of your web project.

We are passionate and take great care to ship high quality deliverables only. We enjoy working with companies who care about their web project.

Services

White label development

Values

Quality deliverables

We set up a strict workflow to ensure we deliver high quality deliverables: continuous training, enforcement of industry best practices, clean and commented code, automated tests, code reviews. We build robust and resilient solutions in order to maximize the return on investment for our clients.

Technological watch

We regularly try out the latest technologies to provide our clients with the best advices regarding technologies and innovative solutions.

Open source

We use existing software components and only develop features specific to your project, thus minimizing development costs. We try to give back to the open source community as much as we can.

The founders' story

Antoine and François are two long time friends who lived in Asia for a while after graduating in France. Primarily based in Beijing, they have been working in the web industry for more than eight years.

Working in a multicultural environment in startups, web agencies or as independant contractors, they have gained a lot of experience in technical leadership and in shipping web projects of any size.

Khawa Karpo is the name of the mountain where Antoine and François decided to join their efforts and found Khawa Technology.
