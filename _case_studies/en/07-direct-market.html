Client:

  • Direct Market is a startup that connects local producers with near supermarkets.
  • Supermarkets can pick products from a custom catalog and place orders.
  • The technical stack is based on Ruby on Rails Spree framework coupled with a React / Redux frontend.

Responsibilities:

  • Consulting: tools and strategy to develop and maintain the customer's e-commerce platform.
  • Project management: weekly meetings with product owner, maintain backlog
  • E-commerce backend development with the Spree framework.
  • E-commerce frontend development with React / Redux.

Completed Tasks:

  • Setup a fresh Spree instance.
  • Customize the Spree application to the client's needs: producers, supermarkets, logistic fees, dm service fees, invoices, credit notes, and more..
  • Provision and deploy servers using Ansible.
