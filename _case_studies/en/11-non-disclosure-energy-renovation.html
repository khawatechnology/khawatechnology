Client:

  • The client is a specialist in energy renovation for buildings.
  • The tech stack is based on the Ruby on Rails framework.

Responsibilities:

  • Development of a WhatsApp bot integrated with the backend

Completed Tasks:

  • The WhatsApp bot guides the user through a scripted journey
  • The WhatsApp bot collects information about the user's housing
  • The WhatsApp bot saves audio and video files sent by the user
  • The information collected by the WhatsApp bot is displayed in the application's admin interface
  • The admin interface allows control over the bot and sending of customized WhatsApp messages
