Client:

  • Babasport is a marketplace for booking activities such as bachelor parties, anniversaries, etc.
  • It's a simple, flexible and economic service that allows everyone to easily book all kinds of events at any time.
  • Babasport is for everyone who is looking online for unique and fun activities.

Responsibilities:

  • Consulting: tools and strategy to develop and maintain the customer's marketplace.
  • Project management: weekly meetings with the product owner, maintain backlog.
  • Backend and frontend development of new features.
  • Update Rails from 4.2 to 7.0

Completed Tasks:

  • Take over an abandoned branch and complete the features.
  • Bugfixes and refactors.
  • Improve memory usage performances.
  • Integrate Algolia for efficient search.
  • Add new features to the application.
